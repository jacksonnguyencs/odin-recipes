Okonomiyaki

This is a delicious and hearty meal that can be customized to your hearts content!

This is a type of dish where you are the recipe master! It is a simple Japanese pancake that is usually fried on a pan or griddle. This is a meal where you can pick the meats and veggies to your liking. I will provide a simple example that is purely customizable in terms of toppings.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Thinly sliced cabbage
  • Okonomiyaki flour mix
  • Furikake
  • Kewpie mayo
  • Okonomiyaki sauce
  • Dried seaweed
  • Green onions
  • Neutral cooking oil

Steps

  1. Sear the thinly sliced beef with cooking oil in a pan. Once there is some nice brown color on the beef, place it into a bowl for the time being.
  2. Create the okonimiyaki batter following the instructions on the package.
  3. Add the cabbage and beef into the batter and thoroughly mix.
  4. Pour oil into the pan just deep enough to where the surface is covered. Then on a medium heat place a layer of the batter mixture and let it cook a few minutes on both sides.
  5. Place the cooked pancake onto a place and add the furikake, Kewpie mayo, okonimiyaki sauce, dried seaweed, and green onions on top as garnish.
