No Noodle Lasagna

A delicious looking lasagna perfect for the bulking season.

This recipe for lasagna will be an unforgettable experience. One that will blow away not only your taste buds, but your families' too.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Ground meat
  • A packet of Paco seasoning
  • Flour tortillas
  • A can of refried beans
  • 4 cups of Colby Jack cheese
  • 1 jar of taco sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9 x 13' casserole dish
  2. Cook the ground beef in a skillet until evenly brown. This should take around 10 minutes. Add the taco seasoning and let the meat render down.
  3. Place 3 tortillas into the casserole dish and spread a layer of refried beans and meat on the tortillas.Then sprinkle cheese on top. Complete a total of 4 layers in the dish. Only use 3 cups of cheese here.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes and sprinkle cheese on top of the cooked lasagna.
